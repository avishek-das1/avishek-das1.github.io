Mechano-Chemical Information Processing in Soft Matter · Durham University

GitHub Scholar Email

Computation peformed by living organisms is unique - it is error prone yet low-energy and robust. How does such intelligence emerge in living matter? Often, the emergence of functional intelligence in biology involves a combination of mechanical and chemical degrees of freedom covering multiple timescales. Why have such dynamics evolved? Are these mechanochemical motifs optimally designed? We use novel theoretical and computational tools to understand mechanochemical information processing in order to uncover its fundamental design principles.
