Mechano-Chemical Information Processing in Soft Matter · Durham University

GitHub Scholar Email

How does intelligence emerge in soft and living matter? The dynamics of information processing in biology often involves a combination of mechanical and chemical degrees of freedom covering multiple timescales. We use novel theoretical and computational tools to understand mechanochemical information processing and uncover its fundamental design principles.
